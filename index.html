Bespoke Vegan cakes!

Vegan cake is cake made without eggs, dairy butter or dairy milk. Each of these standard ingredients is easily replaced by plant-based substitutes, such as banana
or flaxseed for eggs, non-trans fat margarine or oils
for butters and plant milks for milk.
